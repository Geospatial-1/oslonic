About Amatinyi Feeds

Your trusted partner in animal nutrition

Amatinyi Feeds is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality animal feeds in Kenya. Established in 2010, we have grown to become a household name among poultry and livestock farmers across the country.

Our feeds are formulated by animal nutrition experts using premium ingredients to ensure your animals receive balanced nutrition for optimal growth, health, and productivity.

We are committed to supporting Kenyan farmers with quality products, expert advice, and reliable delivery services.
